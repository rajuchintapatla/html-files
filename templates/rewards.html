There are four kinds of badges: bronze, silver, gold, and platinum, corresponding to the various difficulty levels of challenges and topics. The badges you have earned are an indicator of how far you have come along your track and how much you have learnt. Coins

Learners earn coins throughout their journey on their track by completing challenges and solving problems. These coins are transferable between learners. Coins can also be used in a variety of ways - from redeeming them to purchase merchandise, to extending your subscription. The more coins you earn, the more fun it gets.

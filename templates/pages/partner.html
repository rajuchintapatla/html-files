WHAT WE OFFER

Hoppercamp offers a variety of plans for schools and other institutions that want to avail our services. We have tracks and courses that are designed keeping in mind the needs of students from grade IV upward.

We believe that programming is for everyone - irrespective of age. Learning Computer Science and how to code can provide young students with skills that will prove necessary at higher stages in their education and life. Given the fast pace of the global shift towards technology, it is never too early to take your first step into the world of computers and software. Programming can enhance critical thinking, logical reasoning, problem solving, and decision making capabilities in students.

Our tracks for students at Hoppercamp will use personalized learning methods that will complement the traditional methods used in teaching Computer Science and programming in schools. The best tool to enhance learning inside the classroom, is learning outside the classroom.

About

OUR STORY

Hoppercamp is a new initiative with one ambition in mind: to help students of all ages and backgrounds with a new approach to learning Computer Science. Every person learns in a way that is unique to them, and we can teach scores of skills through programmes that will suit every student’s requirements.

At Hoppercamp, we believe that knowledge is the most important tool anyone can equip themselves with, and real knowledge is a challenge, for both students and teachers. We started out to take on this challenge, because we believe in the value of lessons taught well. Computer Science is becoming more and more crucial as we move closer and closer towards a digital world. The skills we seek to spread are becoming increasingly valuable.

We are a bunch of people who are passionate about Computer Science. We have seen the sheer beauty of the subject, and have been fascinated by it. We want to help others make the most out of it that they possibly can, and in the best way that they possibly can. It’s never too late, and no challenge is too daunting.

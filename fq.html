FAQ's

+ 1.What is Hoppercamp?

Hoppercamp is an educational enterprise aimed at spreading knowledge about Computer Science. We offer a carefully designed, flexible curriculum that focuses on giving learners a clear understanding of fundamentals, as well as the skills required to make a real-world impact.

2. How is Hoppercamp different from other educational websites?

Hoppercamp's curriculum has been designed keeping in mind the fast pace of today's world, and can be customized to fit any schedule. The lessons are organized into tracks, which consist of various courses, which in turn consist of various topics. Learners can pick the lessons that interest them, and work on them at their own pace. Hoppercamp also offers unique and innovative challenges, as well as an efficient reward system.

3. Who is eligible to enroll?

Absolutely anyone! Whether you are a newcomer to the world of Computer Science, or an experienced programmer looking to brush up on some skills, Hoppercamp will provide you with everything you need.

4. What is a track?

A track is a collection of courses that deal with different but related subjects. A course in turn is a collection of different topics studied under the same subject.

Knowledge, skill, expertise, as well as a reliable proof of your competence. There is much to gain by actively participating in the track(s) of your choice.
